Purpose of the ProjectRAE-CRL (Cognitive Research Loop) is a **scientific research overlay** built on top of the RAE (Reflective Agentic-Memory Engine).Its purpose is to:- preserve **continuity of scientific reasoning** over long-term research,- capture **decisions, assumptions, and reflections** that are normally lost,- support **iterative, high-cost, high-complexity research workflows**,- remain **domain-agnostic**, offline, and deterministic.RAE-CRL is **not**:- an AI system for generating hypotheses,- an expert system,- a domain-specific research tool.RAE-CRL is a **cognitive memory scaffold for researchers**.---## 2. Design Principles (Non-Negotiable)1. **Domain Agnosticism**   - No biological, chemical, physical, or engineering logic is hardcoded.   - All domain-specific information lives exclusively in metadata.2. **Decision-Centric Memory**   - The primary unit of value is *why a decision was made*, not raw data.3. **Determinism First**   - RAE-CRL must work fully without any LLM.   - LLMs (if used) are optional UX helpers only.4. **Offline-First**   - No cloud dependency.   - No network access required.5. **Low Interaction Cost**   - Must support CLI/TUI usage.   - Must tolerate incomplete or imperfect inputs.---## 3. Positioning Relative to RAERAE-CRL is an **overlay**, not a modification.- RAE remains responsible for:  - memory persistence,  - retrieval,  - reflection mechanisms,  - time-aware context linking.- RAE-CRL is responsible for:  - defining *what* is stored,  - structuring research artifacts,  - enforcing semantic consistency of the research loop.---## 4. Core Concept: Research ArtifactsRAE-CRL models scientific work as a set of **explicit research artifacts**.### Mandatory Artifact Types1. Hypothesis  2. Assumption  3. Experiment  4. Observation  5. Decision  6. Reflection  These artifacts are:- universal across scientific disciplines,- explicit representations of scientific reasoning,- first-class objects in memory.---## 5. Universal Artifact SchemaAll artifacts share the **same structural schema**.```yamlid: &lt;unique&gt;type: Hypothesis | Assumption | Experiment | Observation | Decision | Reflectiontitle: &lt;short human-readable summary&gt;description: &lt;free-form text&gt;context:  project_id:  research_phase:  related_artifacts: []metadata: {}created_at:author:confidence: optionalNotes:metadata is the only extensible, domain-specific field.RAE does not interpret metadata; it stores and indexes it.related_artifacts explicitly encodes reasoning links.6. Cognitive Research Loop ModelRAE-CRL models research as a non-linear loop:Hypothesis   ↓Experiment   ↓Observation   ↓Decision   ↓Reflection   ↺ (revision of Hypothesis or Assumptions)Key properties:No enforced ordering.Partial loops are valid.Failed or abandoned paths are preserved.7. Typical Research Use CasesRAE-CRL supports:long-running experimental programs,parameter-heavy experiments,multi-person research environments,environments with delayed feedback (e.g., chambers, reactors).The system must allow queries such as:“Have we encountered a similar decision before?”“Why was this approach abandoned?”“Which assumptions repeatedly failed?”8. CLI-First Interaction ModelRAE-CRL must expose a minimal CLI interface.Example Commandsrae crl add hypothesisrae crl add experiment --from hypothesis:H12rae crl add observation --from experiment:E34rae crl add decision --from observation:O21rae crl link D45 H12rae crl query &quot;similar decisions under high temperature&quot;The CLI:must be scriptable,must be usable in laboratory conditions,must not require GUI interaction.9. Optional LLM Integration (Strictly Bounded)LLMs may be used only for:normalizing natural language input,paraphrasing descriptions,improving readability of outputs.LLMs must:run locally,be replaceable,have no write access to RAE memory,have no authority over decisions or linking.RAE-CRL must remain fully functional without any LLM.10. Hardware and Platform TargetsPrimary target:Linux (Arch-compatible)CLI/TUI environmentSecondary targets:portable executionlow system overheadno GPU requirement11. Extensibility GuaranteesThe system must allow:new artifact types (without breaking existing ones),new metadata fields,integration with external tools (ELN, notebooks, version control).Backward compatibility is mandatory.12. Explicit Non-GoalsRAE-CRL does NOT:generate scientific conclusions,validate correctness of research,optimize experimental parameters,replace human judgment.RAE-CRL augments memory and reflection, not intelligence.13. Evaluation CriteriaSuccess is measured by:reduction of repeated failed experiments,improved traceability of decisions,faster orientation in complex research histories,adoption by multiple researchers without customization.14. Summary StatementRAE-CRL is a cognitive infrastructure for scientific continuity.It preserves the history of thinking, not just results.---## Odpowiedź na Twoje pytanie „czy to trudne?” — już po dokumencieNie.
